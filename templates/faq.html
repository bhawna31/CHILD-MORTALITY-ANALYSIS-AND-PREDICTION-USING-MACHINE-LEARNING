Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to understand this healthcare analytics project.

📌 Which dataset is used?

WHO Global Health Estimates Dataset covering 194+ countries and multiple years.

🤖 Which machine learning models are used?

LSTM, Random Forest, and XGBoost.

📊 Why combine Random Forest and XGBoost?

To improve prediction stability, reduce bias, and increase forecasting accuracy.

📈 What is LSTM used for?

LSTM handles time-series forecasting and long-term mortality trend prediction.

🚨 How is risk classified?

Predictions are categorized into High, Medium, and Low risk based on mortality severity.

📄 Can reports be downloaded?

Yes, PDF prediction reports are generated automatically.

🌍 Real-world applications?

Healthcare planning, policy development, child mortality prevention, and global health forecasting.
